TYPES OF BIODIVERSITYt

Genetic diversity

Means:

It can be understood as the diversity of genes within a particular species. This diversity ensures that some species can survive disruptions.

Species diversity:

Means:

It refers to the variety of species within a particular geographical region.

Ecosystem

Means:

It refers to the diversity of different biological communities or ecosystems like forests

Community diversity

Means:

As the ecosystem changes, species best adapted to that particular ecosystem becomes predominant
